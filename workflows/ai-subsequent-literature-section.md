# Workflow: Add "Subsequent Literature" section (AI-assisted)

**Purpose:** Use AI to generate a summary of papers that cite your paper.

---

## Steps

1. In Cursor, switch to **ChatGPT 5.3 mode** (or the current best model).
2. Ask Cursor:
   > "Add a 'Subsequent Literature' section to this MyST document with a one- or two-paragraph description of the papers that have cited the paper in question."
3. Review and accept the generated content.
