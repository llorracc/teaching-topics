# Assignment: PaperPile bibliography capture

**Due:** Before Class 03

---

## Task

Use PaperPile to compile a bibliography for the paper you chose from Econ-ARK "ballpark."

All citations relevant to that paper should be captured in your PaperPile library/database, then exported for submission.

---

## What to submit

- **Preferred:** A BibTeX export (`.bib`)
- **Also acceptable:** RIS export (`.ris`)

**File naming:**
```
<your_last_name>__ballpark__bibliography.bib
```

**Example:**
```
Zhang__ballpark__bibliography.bib
```

---

## What should be in the bibliography

- **The chosen paper itself**
- **The papers it cites** (as captured in PaperPile)
- Any additional sources you decide are important background for understanding/replicating the chosen paper
