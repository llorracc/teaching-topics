## Workflow sequence

### 1. [Add yourself as coauthor](https://llorracc.github.io/workspace-course-topics/workflows/add-coauthor-to-notebook.html)

Add your name to the notebook; save.

**Key points:**
- Find the authors cell (usually near the top)
- Add yourself following the existing format
- Save the notebook (Cmd+S / Ctrl+S)

**Deliverable:** Notebook shows you as a coauthor

---

## Checkpoint

Before proceeding, verify:
- [ ] Your name appears in the authors section
- [ ] Notebook is saved
- [ ] `git status` shows the notebook as modified
